Meet Our Team

Get to know the dedicated individuals who make up the Cryptid Securement Foundation!

Steve Guy - Lead Field Agent

Steve joined CSF in 1996 and has been instrumental in cryptid securement operations across North America. With over 20 years of experience, he’s a trusted veteran and team leader. Steve’s passion for cryptid studies began as a young boy, inspired by stories of Bigfoot. He has one son, Damian, born in 1998, who shares his father’s curiosity for the unknown.

Alexis Bright - Cryptozoologist

Lex, a former biologist, has been with CSF since 2001. Known for her meticulous documentation, she has compiled CSF’s largest database of reported sightings. Lex specializes in aquatic cryptids and has led securement missions near Loch Ness and Lake Champlain.

Hank Johnson - Securement Specialist

Hank is the muscle of the team, a former Navy SEAL who brings strength and discipline to CSF operations. Since joining in 1990, Hank has become legendary for his bravery, especially during the 1998 GYG-131 encounter, where he kept his cool under extreme circumstances.

Felicity Nguyen - Lead Research Scientist

Dr. Nguyen holds a PhD in cryptozoology and paranormal studies and has been with CSF since 2000. She leads research efforts on cryptid biology, helping to unravel the mysteries behind cryptid behavior and physiology. Her current focus is on hybrid species within the Appalachians.

Morgan Tran - Communications Director

Joining in 1994, Morgan manages CSF’s communication efforts, ensuring the organization remains out of the public eye. She coordinates with local authorities to keep cryptid sightings under wraps and leads training on CSF’s non-disclosure policies.
