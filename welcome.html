About CSF

Founded in the late 20th century in response to a rising number of unusual sightings and unexplainable deaths, the CSF now operates globally, maintaining hidden research facilities, secure containment sites, and elite field teams.

Mission Statement

The CSF exists to ensure that humanity remains uninformed and safe from creatures and phenomena beyond comprehension. Their mission is to "Contain, Secure, Protect." The organization's ultimate goal is not just to neutralize potential threats but to study and understand these entities to prevent future breaches and to control their unpredictable powers..
