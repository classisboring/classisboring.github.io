About CSF

The Cryptid Securement Foundation (CSF) is dedicated to the securement, protection, and study of cryptids worldwide. From Bigfoot to the Loch Ness Monster, CSF has been working in the shadows to keep humanity safe and cryptids secure since 1994. With a team of passionate cryptozoologists, the CSF is on a mission to understand and protect these mysterious beings from public interference and potential harm.

Mission Statement

To uncover, document, and protect cryptids worldwide while keeping their existence hidden from public knowledge.
